Hello everyone,

This is {HELLS ELITE} Thorofin, one of the founding members of {HE}. I've been playing hl2dm for a couple of years now, but hadn't really done much FPS gaming since Wolfenstein, Hexen & Decent. While I'm not bad, I am definitely just an enthusiast, and do the best I can with the limited time I have to play.

I've been the admin behind the {HE} servers, as well as helping out people from [GGG], -=DMS=-, & anyone else who wanted information on setting up a server & adding Mani's plugin. (BTW Kudos to Keeper for planning to integrate admin functions to the Headshot plugin).

I also setup & manage the Hells Elite website & forums http://www.hellselite.net. While it's not fancy, it does get the job done.

I do mapping as well, but due to my time constraints and my IRL job, I tend not to have the time for mapping from scratch, so I tend to Mod other maps. Some of you may have played the double sized version of aim_arena_b4 that I put together.

Anyhow, I'm glad Holymoly pointed me this direction, and I look forward to learning & contributing what I can to the community.
