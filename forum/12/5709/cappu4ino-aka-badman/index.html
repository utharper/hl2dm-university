Introducing:
Hello everybody, my name is Boris, nickname Cappu4ino (in russian language first letter of 4 is ch, so this nickname reads as "Cappuccino") aka Badman.

I was born in 1991, so I'm 19 years old now.

I started to play HL2:DM in 2005-2006. This game was purely fun for me, so there was now serious attitude from me.
Simple day, 2006, September:
I'm join public server that running dm_overwatch, 10-12 players, and I get the top among all this people.
So I was very naive, and I ran to the "Stream arena" ( it was very popular Russian hl2dm forum in 2005-2007) forum, to brag about my "skill".

After my unsuccessful gaming practice, I started to engage in mapping.

CHAPTER ONE
"Constructive thinking"

When I was about 2-3 years our family was living hubly. So they couldn't buy to me any cool toys, or to make me a lot of presents.
But once day, they presented me Lego game designer.
It was a big kit for making buildings. So it was my basic occupation till I seven years old.
Construction became my favorite thing, so I caused my interest to the mapping and level design.

In the end of 2007 I started to learn source mapping. I made maps for HL2, I didn't published them, it was only for me. Later I started to make maps for hl2dm.
I was a kid, so I not taken root in hl2dm society, and players were not delighted by my maps.
Ragequit from HL2DM world and Source mapping.

In the beggining of 2008 I started to learn Sandbox 1\2 (used to create maps for the CryEngine: FarCry, Crysis).
It is better then source sdk, because it have real-time rendering, and you can test your maps without entering any game.
It was a lot of fun again, but it became not so interesting as it was earlier.

Then I started to leave CryGames, and I forgot about my PC and any games for some time.

CHAPTER TWO
"Mapping put me on the game"
Year 2010. I came back to my PC. I was customising my desktop, and I found cool wallpapers with "Krak des Chevaliers" castle in Syria.
It was amazing for me, and very interesting to visit it, to look on it with my own eyes.
Subsequently I decided to create a map with catle theme. Only Source Sdk knowleges could let me to realise my idea.
I started to make dm_undercastle. I was spending a lot of my time to this map, and sometimes I was very tired because of it.
And once day, I visited hl2dm public, I just wanted to relax. I was visited in the "Warriors of 3D clan" on this public. After I entered it, players there, started to train me hard.
I started to use my brain in games, and left behind my public games, so I played only 1v1, 2v2, 3v3 and 4v4.
So I found players who will help me to test my maps, in that way I met aforizm, he was playing in Lsd at that time.
He helped me to make a lot of maps, so thanks to him.
We published dm_undercastle_r1 on HL2DMU, CU, FPS banana, and after people said that they like it, I decided to continue my mapping growth.

I played HL2DM, started to play in CU 1v1, met some interesting people like Abnoksius (don't sure about this is right name), Swot, haL.
I have a lot of good maps, and some of them are in CU maplists now.
I like this game, and don't want to leave it.
Glad to meen all you here, thanks for you to playing this game, we'r family, we are part of this virtual world.

Best regards, Badman.
GLHF
