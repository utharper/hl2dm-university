Welcome to the site!

I've played a couple variations of nokill servers. Build servers, puzzle servers, and co-ops they can be a great break from fragging. esspecially the puzzle maps. They have a bunch of huge rooms (usually around 15) and each one has a different puzzle that needs to be completed before the next room can be opened. Really fun stuff, all the puzzles are extremely creative and vary quite a bit in their objectives and respective challenges.

One thing I think hl2dm is really missing is jump maps. In counterstike 1.6 they had these maps that were completely based around making technical strafe jumps to ascend up the map. Each map usually resembled a mountain in some way and the only way to get up the mountain was to follow the path which consisted of strafe jumps which got progressively harder as you moved higher on the map. A great way to perfect the distance and accuracy of strafe jumps.
