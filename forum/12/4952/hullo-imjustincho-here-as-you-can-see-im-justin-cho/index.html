Gravity gun jump, strafe as if you were going to strafe jump, but strafe toward the physic then primary fire with your grav gun when directly over the physic, if it's flat you don't even have to jump over it just step on it and launch, other wise jump over it and launch when directly over.

Phys jump, grab a prop and push it into the wall or floor, then let go of it and jump. (this works when a physic clips into your player model)
