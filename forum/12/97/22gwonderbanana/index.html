Hi all

I usually play as Wonderbanana or Nana (or some fruity variation!) and I am a member of the 22nd Global Gaming Clan, or 22g as its known. The clan website can be found here: http://22g.co.uk/forum/index.php

22g is a community of gamers (most HL2DM based) with an emphasis on promoting a fun based enviroment suitable for players of all skill and free from harassment. 22g currently have 5 popular HL2DM servers and one for CSS.

I've been playing DM for quite a while now, and originally was attracted to 22g as initially I found some servers I'd been on before rather abusive to new players (i nearly gave the game up because of this).

Since then though, I've abandoned my water wings and now do my best to help others in the same way I was helped when I started. And on that note, I think the site you have here is a superb idea and fantastic for the community, nice one and good luck with it!
