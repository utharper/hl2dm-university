Hello everyone, my name is Agent Graves, but I went through countless different usernames before, I'm pretty sure I played with many of you at some point within the last few years.

Before becoming a HL fan in 1998 and being hooked on the series and it's mods and conversions ever since, I started out with the brilliant Dukematch and was there with Q2 and Q3A. I also sticked with UT for some time, but the most and the most memorable time I spent with HL's dm.
Having played HLDM to death I was at first dissapointed at Valve for giving us CS:S (I started disliking CS around 1.3) with HL2, and then again for giving the fans a seemingly inferior dm game with lot's of bugs and little support.

So I played it, left it for something else, came back, left shortly after, came back again, left again. A.s.f..
But now, having realized that I keep returning to this game because of it's sheer greatness, I want to stay.

And I'm glad Valve's little loved bastard child has found a new home with what seems a great and friendly community. My respect for the University, this is a place and community the King of all DM games deserves.

I'm here for the fun, I like to laugh (a lot) in the game, but I've also come to a point where I'd like to learn all the advanced movements, as it is something I never bothered to pick up.
I'll be looking forward to see you guys on the servers and have some fun with you. HL2DM style.

Thank you for reading, hope to frag (or be fragged by) you soon,

Agent Graves
(a.k.a Aunt Gravy, hangOVER, -LennoX-)



P.S.: greetings to anyone who knows me from the Eyeclans SH-server, that place showed me what how great the HL2DM community can actually be.
