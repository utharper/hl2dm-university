the X-Gamers-Clan was started on Feb. 2nd, 2007.
We are a Half-Life 2 clan and we welcome all to join.

We have the following common sense rules for this clan:
1. No swearing on the servers is permitted.
2. No use of offensive player names or tags will be allowed.
3. No use of porno or any other type of offensive sprays will be tolerated.
