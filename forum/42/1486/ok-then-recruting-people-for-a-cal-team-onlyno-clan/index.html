OK Pomp, As a new CAL player here is what I have learned so far. Hopefully this helps you some.

Developing a CAL team is gona take a lot of time and hard work.
The team needs to practice and play together ALOT.
The team needs to have schedules that match often to allow for practicing and playing ALOT
You need to learn every inch of every CAL map including weapons, health, suit and spawn locations
You need to come up with a names for the different areas on each map so that the team knows who is where
You need to come up with several strategies (both offensive and defensive) for each map
Your team needs to learn to communicate effectively
You should expect to die a lot learning how to play with teams that have been together for years and have all of the above memorized plus have skill levels many times greater than your team.

Thats pretty much what I have figured out so far....I am finding that its a rather large undertaking and a bit overwhelming.
