The rocket room on avalon is extremely vulnerable to orbs and is generally fairly easy to clear out, if not hold. The inner part of zeta is vulnerable to orbs as well, and quick snipes. The ar2 and rpg are also both reasonably easy to steal if the inner team doesnt control them well, and the charger is workable from inside or out.

Neither map is unbalanced to anywhere near the degree that they need to be removed from the league, and besides, the perfectly balanced map doesnt exist. I havent seen a map yet in cal that doesnt get criticism from someone at some time.

Taking the rocket out of cal wouldnt make the play any better or more skill intensive. Most maps have only a single rpg compared to multiple of everything else- some dont have one at all- and all of those maps have ways to combat or take back the rpg.

Anyway, those scores were nothing to get worked up over... they were fairly close, but in the end, someones gotta lose.
