Ko-Tao

2008-06-14 01:34:35

The main season has kicked off. The default match time this season is each Tuesday at 9:00 PM EST, with playoffs running twice weekly on Monday and Thursday. Check the schedules or your match sidebar for week-1 match listings.

All players who had either 2 forfeit losses or unreported matches during the preseason have been set to inactive status. PM the 1v1 division manager listed on the admin sidebar if you were purged and wish to be reactivated; however, note that this priviledge is only available once, and players who are purged a second time due to inactivity will not be readmitted to the division until the season is over.

Lastly, the 1v1 division is now closed for new signups. The division will begin accepting new signups again at the end of the regular season.
