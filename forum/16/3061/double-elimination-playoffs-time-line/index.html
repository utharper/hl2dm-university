In the past we have run double elimination playoffs in 3 weeks. However with open and main divisions now it is likely that more active teams will make playoffs and the desire to complete playoffs sooner will be less of an issue. Would you like to schedule a double elimination playoff to last just 3 weeks with 2 matches per week, or 5 or 6 weeks with 1 match per week except the finals. Paradox

2009-01-11 06:07:33

Just for a little bit more explanation and for the sake of the discussion.

To schedule 5 leagues 1v KB, 2v KB, Reg 1v, reg 2v and reg 4v with 2 matches per week is going to be crazy if people are participating in both.

We will have to schedule some of the playoffs for these leagues on the same days

Just as an example in past seasons with double elim and 2 matches per week:
Reg 1v would be Monday and Thursday
Reg 2v would be Tuesday and Friday
Reg 4v would be Wedsday and Saturday

So this leaves the question: What to do with the 2 KB leagues?

One idea we were kicking around was
kb 1v Monday and Thursday
KB 2v Tuesday and Friday

Or reverse them to

KB 1v Tuesday and Friday
KB 2v Monday and Thursday

The reason we are thinking of avoiding the days for 4v4 is because we all know how hard it can be to get 4 players together as it is so we thought it might be best to avoid hangups with those days.

Please discuss constructive ideas and suggestions

Thanks
