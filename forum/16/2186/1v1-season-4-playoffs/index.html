1v1 Season 4 Playoffs

Ko-Tao

2008-08-03 05:19:35

The 1v1 division S4 playoffs will commence on Friday, August 8th. The format will be Top 8, Double Elimination.

All pending regular season matches and makeups must be reported by 11:59 PM PST Wednesday, August 6th. Matches still unreported past this date will be marked as double forfeit losses.

As a reminder, the points system used to determine playoff eligibility and seedings is as follows:
Win: 4 Points
Tie: 3 Points
Loss: 2 Points
Forfeit Win: 1 Point
Forfeit Loss: 0 Points

Finally, SourceTV demo recordings will be mandatory for the Championship Match.
