Playoff format will be 4 weeks of double elimination play, with a final week for the Championship Match.

For players not familiar with the double elimination format, the first match loss sends the loser to the lower bracket, while a second match loss removes them from the playoffs.

Should a player have a flawless upper bracket performance, they will skip week 4 and move directly to the championship bracket as high seed. In the final match, low seed needs to win 2 match sets against high seed, while high seed only needs to win a single match set to take the Championship.
