The new HL2:DM Warzone site gives a new refreshing look to the start of its second season. Last season 8 teams competed in 3v3 Warzone with {EE} coming out victorious. Now that both leagues are out of the picture Warzone looks to pick it up yet this time on a whole new level. Good Luck to all participants this season and the Warzone Team can't wait for another fun and exciting season in the competitive world of Half Life 2 Deathmatch!
