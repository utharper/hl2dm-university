L2k

2008-05-09 03:02:27

I'd like to see Zeta either fixed for competitive purposes or removed all together.

The inside is way to powerful (rpg, ar2 and orbs,smg nades, shotguns and tons of armor) and there are 2 spawns inside.
From my experience playing zeta, the match is often decided by the person or teams that get either the inside spawn or can get inside first. The rest of the match becomes a non stop king of the hill rpg campfest, and too bad if this is not your style your gonna lose if you don't do it.

Due to the inside spawns the other person or team stuck outside are at a great disadvantage based on the luck of the first spawn and I don't feel this is what a CAL map/match should be like. The are no other maps in CAL that work this way, and I don't think Zeta should either. The fact that someone can spawn inside and someone can spawn far outside means that it is not a fair race to the rpg from the get go, and the only way this map is ever played is by racing then camping rpg.

I really don't think king of the hill maps (they always promote camping over skilled play) have any place in CAL in the first place but if the map were to be fixed I'd say make all spawn points a equal distance from rpg, get rid of inside spawns and put a AR2 outside as to make use of the many orbs which are already outside.

I will make another post about replacement map suggestions later although I can say I'd play caverns over zeta any day.
