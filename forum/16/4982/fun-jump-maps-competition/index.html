Hello all HL2DM players,

We (Lich and Silla) decided to make a fun competition for the jump maps enthusiasts to see who is the fastest player of several jump maps.
This is a fun competition, the prize will be your own sense of reward

For the matter of this event we created a website with all related information and the maps in which the competition will take place.

The competition dates are still to be announced, but will be so very shortly. During the time of its duration you can provide as many races on as many maps you want. There is no limits to your attempts on being the fastest one. Finally at the end of competition we will publish a ranking based on the fastest races.

The winner demo of each map will become a video as prize, where everyone can see his jumps and may receive many compliments ^^ We are not commercialy organized ... so no cars or bunnies as gift ...

Go check it out:
http://racejumper.hl2dm.net

Have Fun!
