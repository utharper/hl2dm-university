As I understand it now, these are the problems:
  1. If a person suicides with a weapon, prop, or other entity it removes one from the team score.
  2. If a person suicides by falling ( worldspawn death ) then it does not remove one from the team score.
  3. If a person kills a teammate, then the player's score goes down and their team scores go up by one.
And these would be the solution:
  1. If a person suicides then their score and the team score should go down by one.
  2. If a person suicides then their score and the team score should go down by one.
  3. If a person kills a teammate, then give the choice whether the team score should go down by one or remain unchanged. But definitely don't go +1.
