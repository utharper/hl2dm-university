Our Clan has been with Art of War Central for as long as I've been playing. We recently developed a horrible lag issue on our St Louis server. All 3 of our servers were on one box, all 3 would spike at the same time with latency reaching the low 300's. Very slowly the spike would fall back into the mid 100's and stay for several minutes before returning to normal. It was cyclic and almost predictable. AOWC told us they saw no issues.

Our Clan Leader finally convinced them to move us out of the St Louis box and into 3 different boxes in 3 different locations. But I think it really took some arm twisting on our part. So maybe avoid AOWC.
