I'm pretty much in love with SourceMod.

From an administrative standpoint: Pros: there are a ton of SM plugins and it seems like you can always find one for exactly what you want to do. Cons: there are a ton of SM plugins and sometimes you have to go hunting or even write your own plugin if you can't find exactly what you're looking for.

From a programming standpoint: Pros: it's fantastic and easy to write new capabilities or mod existing ones. Cons: the language, SourcePawn, is too simple, to a fault, and makes ordinarily simple things complicated. (data structures mostly)

For me the Pros far outweigh the Cons.
