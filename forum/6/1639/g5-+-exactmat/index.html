The G5 is a true laser mouse not an optical mouse often called a laser mouse. It is well known that they do not do well under low sensitivity situations because the maximum acceleration can be exceeded and it will skip. The only solution is to buy a different mouse the MX518 is the current best Logitech offering and it was won some tests for maximum speed it can still track at.
