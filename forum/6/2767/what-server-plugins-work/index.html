Zman42 wrote:for the love of all that is decent, don't use spawn protection.
Yeah I'm actually debating this at the moment.

Quake 3/Live has a form of spawn protection. You start off with 120 life when you spawn, and it slowly ticks down to 100 (everything above 100 always ticks down). This makes it so you can't be spawn raped by a single shot. In Quake Live rail damage is even reduced to 90 to cut back more on insta-spawn rape.

UT3 has a 3 second spawn protection. You take no damage whatsoever for the 3 seconds, and you glow like a mofo.

HL2dm is the only one that doesn't offer anything, but its also the only one you spawn in with insta-kill weapons too, atleast outside of the Impact Hammer in UT3.

I wasn't thinking of anything drastic like what [FF] has, where you are constantly protected until you move, and people take 10 points of damage if they attack you anyway. Thats too easily abused, as I've seen players use spawn protected people as shields, while the attacker loses health as his shots hit the protected player instead.

If anything it would be a 2 second spawn protection from damage only. No other rules. However, I've never been a fan of spawn protection so I'm not 100% sold yet.
