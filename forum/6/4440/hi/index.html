I would contact your ISP and see if they can test the connection. Do you have cable or DSL?

If you have cable, most of the time a bunch of houses are connected to the main line through the same junction box/connection. If alot of your neighbors are on the internet at the same time it can slow down your connection. If thats the case, not much you can do.

If its DSL, I would call the DSL company and have them test the line. Try resetting your modem.

If you have wireless, your just fucked and unless you can get a wire to the modem there isnt anything you can do with that either.

Also check your computer for spyware, that can slow down your computer's communication with the internet real bad and it acts like lag.
