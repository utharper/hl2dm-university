Download links: Mediafire.com, Narod.ru, depositfiles, WebFile.

Installation: Make the backup folders for your: scripts, resource and materials. Delete your old folders and start the game (to make them default) before the extraction of new ones. Extract the folders scripts, resource and materials from the archieve into half-life2 deathmatchhl2mp according to your monitor size (4-3, 16-9, 16-10). You can copy all files.ttf from "resource" folder into windowsfonts (optional).

Uninstall: Delete folders: scripts, resource and materials from the "hl2mp" folder. And put your backup folders

note about hud: Tested on 4:3, 16:9, 16:10. Works on any steam version.
