Jelly Fox wrote:What causes missing explosions? is that server related or a bug?
Lack of gpu resources to render them, usually caused by visually overdone and/or poorly optimised maps (excessive particle effects in one visleaf etc). A stronger card can overcome this to an extent, but its fairly easy for a greedy or bad mapper to take the bsp to the point of no return where no level of pc power can counter the sheer amount of crap that wants to be rendered simultaneously (much of which- like explosions, for example- wont be rendered at all due to either lack of resources or engine limitations).
