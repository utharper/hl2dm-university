My recommendations:

* Even if you use a specific crosshair in game, replace it with a single dot for frag videos with no brackets. This is the least distracting while still showing where the shot is.

* Try not to run same maps together. Mix up the maps for more variety and to keep it interesting.

Sequence following is just mag kills and looks like average gameplay. I would cut this one.
