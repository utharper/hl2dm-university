Footage export sound problem resolution

Walking Target

2007-02-18 18:48:57

My frag video has been on hold because every time I exported the footage the sound file was empty, or would not export.

Turns out my sound was set to 5.1 surround and it cannot be exported this way.

Specifically it was set at:

snd_surround_speakers = 5

Fixed by changing to stereo:

snd_surround_speakers = 2

Maybe this will help someone else with this problem.
