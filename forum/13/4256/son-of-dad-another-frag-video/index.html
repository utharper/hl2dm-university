Hey guys, this is my first attempt at a frag video.

Many of you won't know me but i'm an old player from Australia. The only exposure i've had is from Holy's Community Video.

Back to the video, my first ever project using Sony Vegas Pro. I had some trouble with the encoding of the file to make it a decent size for you guys to download. I'm not a pro at Sony Vegas, so i was learning along the way but the tutorials on youtube helped alot.

My thoughts on this video is just a compilation of how i played back then (which isn't really that long ago) but it was effective for a long time. So instead of having ricochetting bolts i've always thought why not just put a bullet in his head. So this video reflects how i play, simple but effective. So not too many effects in the video but i tried my best to sync it

These frags were mostly taken from the 1v1 tournament we had on dm_lockdown. Therefore, its kinda short and sweet because everyone gets sick of the same map.

So here it is: http://www.filefront.com/14025569/SonofDad.mp4

I really would appreciate some feedback as i'm looking forward to making frag videos in TF2 now.

Shoutouts to all the players from AUS, made the dodgy game that was HL2DM into something really enjoyable. Shoutout to Holymoly, Onti, and DarkSoul, even though you guys don't know me, your frag videos were great and really helped in how i shaped this one.

Hope you guys enjoy!
