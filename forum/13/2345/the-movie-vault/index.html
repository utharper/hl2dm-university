poconut

2008-08-25 02:13:54

Here are the ones that I thought of.
Ode to hl2dm
Art of War
Finality
Game Over
Unwonted
Conflict's Video

There are other good ones, but I find that these have unique value.

Ode to hl2dm captures (at least in me) those little quirks that make hl2 a great game. It's like a hl2dm dream, a good dream that makes you happy and sad at the same time.

Art of War simply has the best editing, period. The frags aren't the best, but they're tasteful and especially impressive looking for new players (not meant to be an insult, it's just that new players can't appreciate certain frags). The music is also great.

Finality is already up there, but it's good because it's of the community. It's like a video "of the players, by the players, for the players."

Game Over is also already up there. My personal opinion, but I think that this is the best video of this game. It has a kill from every weapon, and clearly demonstrates advanced techniques. If I was going to show a new player the potential in this game, I would show them this video.

Unwonted is on there, and it's also great because it shows the potential for greatness in the game, especially with grenade jumping. I would think that this video would catch the eyes of quake and unreal players because of all the in-air action.

Finally, conflicts video. I consider it the most underrated hl2dm movie. It has everything required of a good frag video, except that the quality is somewhat lacking. But what compensates for the quality is the music selection. I tried experimenting with nonconventional music in my videos, but with no success. Conflict's vid takes a different approach to the music, and it turned out perfect. I can't explain it, so I won't try to. It is legend.


So yeah, those are my choices for a hall of fame.
