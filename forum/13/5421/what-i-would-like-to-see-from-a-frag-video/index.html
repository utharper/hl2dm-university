I might as well give opinion on what I like to see in frag movie.

Focusing on content solely what I like to see is intelligent play more than anything else. So what I mean by intelligent play well what I don't mean is luck shots. For example throwing a nade half way across the map randomly or shooting at the same spot multiple times with a xbow hoping for a kill. Let face xbow bounce are so not interesting any more since after 'game over' unless they are purely reaction based or on the fly thinking. You know what I mean those countless frag movies with the typical drawn out rail cam shot. So what would be intelligent play well simply out smarting you opponent predicting where they are going to go or what they are going to do. Since this game has so many methods of killing with physics and grav gun opportunities are endless which is a great way to show skill and technique.

That brings me to skill and technique. I don't watch frag movies to see talentless nonames racking up kills with no skill. I want to see players top of their game using bhop movement and fast reflexes and deadly aim. In this game skill and technique don't always win you games it more of who has rpg the most or camps the best or spams the most. Frags movies are a chance to show off your skill it is no means a way of showing how much of a winner you are. I fully admit that I am guilty of trying to get kills in the game in the hardest way possible if I got a rpg and mag im most likely going to use the mag and try and pull of a impossible shot but that just the sort of player I am. Thankfully there are Frag movies

On kill sprees just like many things is depends. Obviously rpg kill after rpg kill is boring so is countless shotgun kills with smg nads. But if it against good opposition like where one player clears area held by the other team with some sick shots and smart play, Its what I would call top draw material for a frag movie.

Also lastly pub footage no matter how good is worthless in my eyes there is no way the viewer knows the content is legit and simply frags against players that know how to play the game is worth so much more.
