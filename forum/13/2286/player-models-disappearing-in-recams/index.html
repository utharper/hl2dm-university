Correct, this is a feature of the source engine. It wont render the player model if it is in certain locations that are out of line of site in order to improve performance and to reduce the consistency of wall hacks.

Record source tv demos whenever you can is the best advice I can give unfortunately.
The frag vid community is pretty used to seeing it tho, so as long as you keep the recam quick, nobody will really mind.
