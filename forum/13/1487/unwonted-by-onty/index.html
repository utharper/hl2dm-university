Well there is one other factor to consider with the RPG, once it gets out of visual contact with the red dot, you lose control of it and it will turn back on you and may even come back in your face if you are not careful. What can happen on curves is you start curving one direction and then you lose guidance and if there is enough flight time after that, the rpg can go in the opposite direction from the one you are pulling it. Sometimes this results in gnarly kills, other times it messes up your curve enough to miss the intended contact point.
