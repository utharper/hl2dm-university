If you are watching a massive file that is not yet compressed, it could just be you cant play it back. Try compressing one with xvid as a test.

If your settings are off, there is a setting in vdub that synchs the audio with the video perfectly. I will post it when I remember where it is.

EDIT: Go to video -> frame rate. Select the option that says "Change so video and audio durations match".

This will synch it perfectly every time.
