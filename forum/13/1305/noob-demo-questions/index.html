Epoch wrote:Alright some of these might be down right laughable

Can you only watch demos from the perspective of the person recording the demo?

If you press SHIFT+F2 you'll open the demoui tool, by pressing the "Drive" button you can hold either your left or right mouse button (can't remember which) and use the WASD keys to maneuver the camera angle, although it is still subject to show your in game movement.

How do I activate the demo hud while im watching a demo?

Pretty sure SHIFT+F2 is for that, unless you want to make the arms,weapon, gloves etc, disappear, then you have to type "cl_drawviewmodel 0" into the console.

When I am recording a demo, when the map changes does the demo stop recording, or does it continue into the next map?

No it doesn't say you name your starting demo "epoch1v1" then every map change it will add a gradual number to seperate them ie "epoch1v1_2" "epoch1v1_3" although if you're like me you'll forget to start recording again after the game crashes and you jump right back in, gets me every time.
