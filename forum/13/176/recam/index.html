Yeah the demo smoothing is buggy. I've been working with it for about 6 months now and when you set a key frame (a position for the camera to follow along a path) the height of your view will never be true with the final result, meaning its always off centered on the Y axis, so you have to adjust and try a few times.

If you're jumping during the demo smooth, when you go into drive, the screen will bounce, a good way I found of getting rid of this is deleting the frames that jump when you extract through console, the frames are fast enough that its not noticeable and looks far better than a jittery camera.
