Then there's also the issue of 3on3 vs 4on4 and FF on or off. The team playing what they're used to will always have an advantage - maybe a 3on3 with FF off could be a middle ground. Probably easier to get 3 players with good connections than 4. Aslo, if you play with high ping and lag I imagine you're happier playing with FF off
