6:45pm - Picks should be going right along. Once a captain is choosen for a team an admin will invite him/her to a Draft team steam group. The server information will be posted there. (Draft team groups will be cleared after every draft)

7:00pm - Teams should be finished picking and the games may start. The server info will be posted to the Draft team steam group pages

Here is a little more info on the Draft.
There is more to follow.

-The Draft will be 4 versus 4 (or 3 v 3, depending on participation) and will be run from Steam.
There are 8 different Draft Team Group pages created for the teams.
http://steamcommunity.com/groups/DMDraftTeam2
Team Captains will be picked (or coerced) and will be admin for their Team that night.

-Heres' how it will work.
All Draft members can check for any schedule changes and general information here.
http://steamcommunity.com/groups/draftg
Draft members will get periodic event updates.

On Draft night, event messages will go out to join this group chat. Team Captains will be decided and while they are picking, each Team Captain (1 - will assigned Admin for their own Draft Team group page, and get an invite for the Team Group. After selection, Team Captains will go to their Team group (Draft Team 5 for example) and they will invite their players and join chat there. On their page, the team will have server info to meet the other team.

Everything will be done in Steam. The Teams will be the only folks invited to their group, so they can use their own chat.

Summary:
Join this Group prior to Draft.
Team Capts will be picked and made Temp admin of their own Steam Team Group.
