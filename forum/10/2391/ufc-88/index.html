Nobody trains a single style anymore, those days are long over. Almost every MMA fighter you see these days has Muay Thai training. The leg kicks and checks, all of the knees, clinch work/pummel/head control, standing elbow techniques all derive from it.

Regarding submissions and street fighting (and don't get me wrong I love bjj and I realize it's value), pure submissions are a backup tool. To ensure your true safety you should remain mobile at all times, try to secure some form of weapon, and if you do take someone down make sure they land hard on something solid like cement, then get back up and kick the shit out of them.
