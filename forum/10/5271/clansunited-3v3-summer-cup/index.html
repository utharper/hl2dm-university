# 1st place: 6 months hl2dm server 8 slots private + 75€

# 2nd place: 3 months hl2dm server 8 slots private + 50€

# 3rd place: 3 months voice server 10 slots private + 25€

The Cup starts at Sunday 15th of August , and there will be 1 Match for each team each week.

Cup System will be like the Football World Cup, so there are poules where every Team faces every other team. First two teams will go to the knock-out Stage and play for the final.

- dm_biohazard_cal

- dm_helix
- dm_lockdown
- dm_lostvillage_r1
- dm_octagon
- dm_qtech_r3
- dm_zeta_rc2
- dm_asylum

Teams chose a map from this maplist before the cup starts. Teams can change their homemap as much as they want before the upcoming round starts. Means, when the round starts on thursday, then teams can change their homemap until wednesday 23.59cet.

End of registration is Friday 13th of August
