It always intrigues me how many people will start screaming "omfg drama! please! please! everyone get along! omfg the world is going to end because of all this drama on forum dedicated to a mostly forgotten but totally awesome fps video game! did i mention stop the drama? please?" but come back day after day to see what will happen next. Society loves conflict (not you, .conflict, the other one, although we love you too, not the gay way, but still, you know what I mean). As much as people say they hate it, and complain about it, it keeps them here - posting, watching, arguing, interested. This site, with all it's useless posts, giant egos and 'omg i hate you assholes i quti' posts is one solidifying point that defines this community. We love some, we hate others, but we've kept coming back, kept playing the game, kept the wheels in motion to keep a game alive that Valve has all but completely forgotten.
The Cafeteria is our home on the internet. It's where we laugh and rage. If this forum was completely on-topic all the time, it would quickly die from the lack of personality and I'm afraid to say that a good percentage of the people playing would fade away as well. So applaud the Cafeteria and it's ramblings, stupidity, arguments, humor-filled and humorless posts. It's keeping the game interesting.
