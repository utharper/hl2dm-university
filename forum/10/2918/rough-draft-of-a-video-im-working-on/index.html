Heh, alright

Bind to whatever your most comfortable to you and with keys as close as possible to your fingers.

For me I use:

F for crossbow
C for grenade
X for the 357 mag
V for the shotgun
G for the RPG

MOUSE4 (another side botton on the mouse ) for SMG
"1" for stun stick/crowbar
SPACE and mousewheeldown for jump


I suggest you make an autoexec.cfg to save all your settings, that way if you crash/reinstall you dont lose your settings.
