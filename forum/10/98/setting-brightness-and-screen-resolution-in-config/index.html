You can't, I know this for a fact, because you can't set the brightness level to anything else but default on dxlevel 80, I've had the same issue a while back, when you change it back to dxlevel 90, you will be able to... If you are running dxlevel 90, just make your config.cfg not read-only, boot up your game, go into your Video Setting, alter the Brightness level to what you want it to be, quti the game and make your config.cfg read-only again. As far as I know there is no command in your config.cfg that's directly connected to the Brightness level. As for dxlevel 80, all the above can't be done, you can try it, but it won't work... Only solution is to change the value everytime you start hl2dm... As for your resolution, same problem occurs, very weird, I assume your running 800x600 at the moment?
Why force a lower dxlevel though if your card can handle a higer level?
