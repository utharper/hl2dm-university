- [HL2] Headcrab: How do you join [BMC]?
- [BMC] Durden: You should probably talk to Sadistical. He's the leader, I'm sure you'd do fine in the tryouts.
- I don't remember the process of getting in too well, other than the naming part. Sadistical: Dude, you're in. Okay, but we need to give you a new name...
- Me: Headcrab? G-Man?
- Sadistical: Nah... Hey, what about HeadJob! Cause of the Headcrab name and cause you get so many headshots. Dude, that's an awesome name! Hahaha!
- [BMC] HeadJob has arrived.

- And thus, perhaps the most fun part of my HL2"DM" (*coughCTFcough*) career begins. Endless pubs in CTF maps and stomps in CTF scrims.

- Join STA league. Demolish the first team. I even remember one of our players, crowbar of death, using the Crowbar only the ENTIRE match and having a positive ratio. The map was on dm_steamlabs.
- Then get wrecked by I think {WRW}. I think ninojman was in it. I forgot. It was on lockdown though. This was our first introduction to "wow, there are even better players and they play dm exclusively, what weirdos." Probably how the "second" generation of VDuS met the clan (Scar, Sadi, Corrosive, Me, Constipator, hopefully not forgetting anyone else...).

- Disbanded into... Cartel (which eventually became Euro lol) and HAX (clan of the 2nd best CTF pub server, but nothing tops the Sniper's Playground with it's default settings, awesome map pool, 16-player size, and just general atmosphere).

- HL2CTF... The best mod ever (but it's not HL2DM, so I won't go over that).

- For both BMC and Cartel, I would make the most elaborate strategies, complete with MS Paint maps, routes, and all this nonsense. Oh my goodness.

- I think we had an 8v8 scrim on Avanti? It was ridiculous. Of course, I used Magnum only.

- Edit: Seagull just reminded me of another epic story: this time taking place in a RP server. I believe it was the usual LBC group with some VDuS (Seagull, Tuna, Const, Scar, et moi). So basically, we raided a RP server and broke one fundamental rule of it: no killing. Despite our inferior weapons, we were able to score some kills, thanks to our DM skills. Oh, and when you die in an RP server, you drop money. We used that money to buy certain goodies, as you may imagine.

The server was in shambles. "omfg no killing!" "WOW FUCKING FAGGOTS" "seriously you guys are gay" "STOP KILLING THIS IS RP NOT FAGGOT KILLBOX". etc. Strangely enough, there was one guy who PM'd us "dont kill me, i'll help you." Intrigued, he would ship us some goodies! RPGs, AR2, Magnum, you name it! "omfg they got guns now!" "wow killers are fucking ruining the game!" "ADMINS FUCKING BAN THEM"

Of course, we weren't technically "breaking" any rules; the no-kill thing was just a matter of courtesy. You could "roleplay" as a gangster/serial killer, but you'd simply end up in jail. Eventually, the townsmen would score their lucky radiator kills or catch us with our pants down (no ammo/reloading/etc.). From here we'd get sent to jail.

Except that the aforementioned helper would come in and lockpick us out of the jail. Yes, you can supposedly "buy" lockpicks and welders and crap like that! So VDuS is free once again, roaming around with Mags and the likes.

The chaos ensues for another 10 or so minutes until we all die. All except for "Charles." The townsmen finally began to stick as a pack, using their superior weapons and wallets to eventually kill us. Together, they decided to escape to the roof for safety.

Me: wow fking vdus fags keep killing me, where r u guyz?
Townsman: follow me, we're gonna go to roof
Town: NO KILLING OK GUYS?

So, with some of VDuS jailed up and the townsmen finally reaching a safe haven, the town was safe. For now.

We finally reached the "roof", where everyone repeated "NO KILLING" and asked "you guyz dont have guns right?"

"Nope."

"cool! we're finally safe from those killfags."

Little did the clumped up townsmen know that beneath my Gravity Gun, I had a RPG filled with three rockets...
