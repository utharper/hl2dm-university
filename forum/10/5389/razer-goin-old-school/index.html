I'd be cautious to get this right away because first gen gaming keyboard, especially mechanical ones, usually have a lot of issues.

Some of my problems with it:

I can't figure out why they chose Cherry Blues. I associate the blues with applications that require tapping keys, like typing and games like starcraft. In dm and other fps you have to hold keys down for movement and crouching. I'll take black or brown for that ty.
Gaming optimized key matrix for minimized ghosting
Most likely means that unless you use wasd for movement you're screwed.

It certainly looks badass though and better than Logitech's keyboards.
