"Throw a toilet at your friend"

These are the words any pr0 dm player (I wouldn't know) loves whispered into their ear as they drift to sleep after six days of gaming and amphetamine pills. "Throw a toilet at your friend through a portal"

So where would that leave the game? Would a portal gun really even be a viable alternative to say, the GG, or could they be used together? Would it fail like someone simply maging your dumbass as you hop through portal to portal in plain view? Could be interesting.
