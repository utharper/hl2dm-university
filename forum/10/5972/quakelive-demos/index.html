Really simple, in game, open console and type /record blablabla. When you want to stop recording, simply type /stoprecord.

The demos will be located in C:\Users\YOURUSER\AppData\LocalLow\id Software\quakelive\home\baseq3\demos

if you want to open a demo and view it, simply create a game and type in console: /demo blablabla.
