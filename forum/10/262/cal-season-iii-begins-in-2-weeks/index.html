CAL Season III Begins in 2 Weeks

Ko-Tao

2007-07-10 14:47:40

CAL-HL2DM season 3 will be kicking off the preseason on Monday, July 23rd.

There are four leagues this season:
1v1 Deathmatch
2v2 Team Deathmatch
4v4 Team Deathmatch
5v5 Capture The Flag

Match format for all leagues will be best 2 out of 3 rounds, with the away team choosing the round 1 map, the home team choosing the round 2 map and the weekly default map being used as the round 3 tiebreaker.

Playoff format in all leagues will be top 8, double elimination. First match loss will send the player or team into the lower bracket; a second loss will remove them from the playoffs.

Rosters are open for signups to all leagues as of now; teams previously entered will not need to re-signup, though any player or team that forfeits both of their matches during the preseason will be deactivated before the regular season starts.

Match dates, maps and mapcycles, rules changes and updated cal.cfgs will be posted within the next 48 hours.
