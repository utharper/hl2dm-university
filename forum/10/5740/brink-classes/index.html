choose class according to your play style..

soldiers
-give out ammo
-plant explosives
-have frag and molotov grenades

medics
-can buff teammates health
-can revive (by throwing a revive syringe)
-can self buff

engineer
-build turrets
-diffuse mines
-buff weapons
-diffuse explosives

operatives
-disguise as an enemy
-spot enemy mines
-hack stuff

All the guns can be used by the 4 classes. However, some guns are dependent on body size.
