This topic is for all the techno addicts that frequent this site, if you haven't stumble upon this site http://www.di.fm/ already here is an official introduction. I just want to share this site with everyone because it is simply awesome. This site started out for the most part as purely a hub for techno and its MANY forms and variations. It is partnered with sky.fm to offer just about all the other genres you could want, they have goapsy trance to free jazz to salsa to classical to world music.

I just recently started to get fervently addicted to this site again, due to my new sound setup which makes the listening experience down right euphoric. Di.fm is the best place I have found for extremely high quality, diverse, internet radio. The variety and quality of tracks in their trance, goapsy trance and chillout sections have kept me listening for years now. All of their free streams are offered in variable bandwidths, but I would suggest the 96kb because the lesser bandwidth streams experience a lot of loss in quality . Tune in and enjoy, I certainly have.

Edit: I can't believe I almost forgot, they also host shows on most of their channels, highlighting the music of a certain dj or collaborators. Some shows are even live performances being put on by the DJ themselves, which is just outstanding as you could imagine.
