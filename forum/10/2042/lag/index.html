Low frames per second causes lag also. If your video card cant keep up with the action, you will lag. Other causes for lag are configuration issues, internet connectivity, server side overload, or server side configuration issues. Usually when I lag, it is server side issues causing me to lag. If you have net graph 3 up, alot of servers can't handle the load they are set at and the update rate (which is probably the most important setting since this updates everyone's position on the map) drops like a rock; sometimes it drops so low on some servers, people are teleporting all over the place or your weapon won't fire because of the excessively low update rate (usually below 15 update rate).
