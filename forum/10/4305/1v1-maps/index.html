Ko-Tao

2009-08-10 05:11:18

If you want other king of the hill type maps, zeta certainly qualifies, and is far more balanced / skill intensive than overwatch as well, not to mention being free of bugs and clipping issues. Biohazard is also mostly control oriented, though not as extremely so as ow/zeta.

Overwatch, aside from its obvious bugs and balance issues (tons of ways to escape the map, almost no life/armor, 90% of spawns in audio/rocket range of the top, etc) just didnt get chosen much in the first 1v1 season, and was accordingly removed. No one asked for it to return (hardly surprising) so it never did.

Tigcrik is generally liked because the play is balanced- the map has varied terrain and fast play without feeling like an arena or killbox, with all the major fps skills (aim, movement, prediction, gathering, control etc) being required to succeed, and every weapon is useful without being overpowered (or is placed according to its overpoweredness, ie rocket etc), the end result being that no singular weapon or tactic dominates the map. Its small size also prevents matches from degenerating into camping / ambushing or a test of who has the better sound setup.
