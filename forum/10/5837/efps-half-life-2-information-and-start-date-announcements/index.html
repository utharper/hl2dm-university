eFPS Half Life 2 Information and Start Date Announcements

Da1

2011-07-06 09:31:13



everythingFPS is pleased to announce that we have a working mod and that we are ready for our first tournament to be hosted! The first tournament will be a single elimination 16-32 team tournament (depending on team sign up amount) ran in Adrenaline Gamer 2 Mod which fixes many issues that plain Half Life 2: Deathmatch has. everythingFPS is changing the way HL2:DM Competitive play is ran in the NA a bit...no don't worry we wont turn friendly fire on but check out the rule updates and important links and dates below.

Rule Changes:
1. Timelimit will be changed from 20 minutes to only 15 minutes.
2. Pause is an option in the event of a player crash.
3. Due to the mod settings sv_pure 1 with a whitelist will be used rather than sv_pure 2.

Important Dates:
July 24th, 2011 @ 11:59 PM PST - Sign Ups close.
July 25th, 2011 @ 12:00 AM PST - Brackets are released.
August 1st, 2011 @ 12:00 AM POST - Round 1 Matches must be completed.
Note: Any incomplete matches after the 1st will be an auto forfeit for both teams and a BYE will be set for the advancing opposing team.

Important Links:
http://www.everythingfps.com - Home Page
http://www.everythingfps.com/section.php?id=3 - HL2 eFPS Fourms
http://www.everythingfps.com/jointournament.php - Join the Tournament
http://www.everythingfps.com/hl2_tourna ... info=rules - Rules
http://www.everythingfps.com/configs/hl2config.zip - Config Pack (Includes the AG2 Client Auto Installer)
http://www.hl2dmpro.com/index.php?site=files&file=172 - AG2 Auto Installer (The Mod you will need to download)

Maps:
Depending on team Sign ups the tournament will be an 8, 16, or 32 team bracket. With this we will need an additional map every time the bracket raises in size because the tournament will be extended a week.

If 8 Team Bracket:
Week 1: dm_lostvillage_r1
Week 2: dm_caverns_r1
Week 3: dm_lockdown_r6

If 16 Team Bracket:
Week 1: dm_lostvillage_r1
Week 2: dm_caverns_r1
Week 3: dm_airfusion_prefinal
Week 4: dm_lockdown_r6

If 32 Team Bracket:
Week 1: dm_lostvillage_r1
Week 2: dm_caverns_r1
Week 3: dm_airfusion_prefinal
Week 4: dm_avalon_b1
Week 5: dm_lockdown_r6
