if you wanna make it look like the person is coming out of the borders (like the border is over the character on the bottom, but he is over the border on top) you could try using masking.
Get both on diferent layers, select the main image and clicking on the border layer with the mask applied to it paint with dark the selected part of the character that you want to be above the border( surely the top). That would "hide" the border, and make it look like the it's below the other image.
