everythingFPS is pleased to announce our first Capture the Flag tournament! The first tournament will be a single elimination 8-16 team tournament (depending on team sign up amount) ran in CTF Mod. Check out the important dates and links below.

Important Dates:
December 8th, 2011 @ 11:59 PM PST - Team Registration Closes.
December 9th, 2011 @ Sometime during the day - Brackets are released.
December 17th, 2011 @ 12:00 AM PST - Round 1 Matches must be completed.

Please Note: Teams will have almost 3 weeks to play their round 2 match due to the holidays, teams are also encouraged to play deeper into the tournament during the holidays if able to do so, but this is not mandatory. Any incomplete round 1 matches after the 17th will be an auto forfeit for both teams and a BYE will be set for the advancing opposing team.
