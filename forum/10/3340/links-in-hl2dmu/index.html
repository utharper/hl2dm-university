Most phpBB installations open new windows/tabs when you click on links in a post. The U is different in this regard. I just got in the habit of holding down <ctrl> when I click on a link on the U (which will cause the link to open in a new tab or window). It may be a setting in phpBB that can be changed but I can't be bothered to look.

In HTML parlance this is controlled by adding a target="_blank" to the anchor and has little to do with your browser or OS (unless the browser manufacturer decides to ignore the standards). In most modern browsers you can control whether _blank is opened in a new window or a new tab.
