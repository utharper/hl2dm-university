How do you know you cannot install the card? Did you try? Since there are no details I will throw in a couple pieces of information.

Power supply out put rating is highly subjective there are lots of PSUs in the low 250 - 350 watt range that ship with OEM machines that out perform 400 - 600 watt PSUs. Basically what I am saying is I would try it out first if you have not already done so. Make sure to connect the 6 pin connectors or molex connectors to the video card and then stress test it in some games. Also what system do you have? what are the specs brand and so on? Some OEM brands do not have standard ATX power connectors and this could really throw a wrench in your spokes.

If money is really that hard to come by you want to exhaust all your options before spending it on a mediocre PSU which may not do anything if your system is CPU limited any way. And probably will not be useful in a future computer build.
