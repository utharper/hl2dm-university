Well, since I can't play hl2dm atm, I tried Medal of Honor. The sound effects were amazing, the cut scenes were pretty well done. The game itself isn't half bad, but it was a lot like the already existing BC2. I really like that it was based on true events, that made the single player mean more to me knowing it was based off real situations and people. What blew it for me was the 5 hour single player, all the damn annoying INVISIBLE WALLS, the crappy team mate AI, the crappy enemy AI, and the single player missions were all extremely linear. You were pretty much a rat going through a maze with no free choice of where to go at all. Multi player only has like 8 maps, and is pretty dense, but fun.

I was really hoping for more. It looks like Black Ops will have more to offer in multi player with every match being automatically recorded and an in game camera editor, a better single player experience that is longer than 5 hours (hopefully) and Black Ops just seems to bring more to the table. More bang for your buck.

Medal of Honor doesn't bring anything new to the Military fps, everything it brings has already been done before. That's my 2 cents.
