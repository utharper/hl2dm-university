BuckyKatt

Reverse spawn killing is when you come out of spawn and kill someone who was gracious enough to let you spawn.

On the FF servers it works this way:

They have spawn protection but if a person spawns in front of you you are obligated to let them run away. If you let them run away (making the mistake of turning your back on them) and they come out of spawn and kill you that is reverse spawn killing. And that is not allowed.

I don't personally like the spawn/reverse spawn rules but I do accept that FF was trying to address a set of issues they perceived to be happening on their servers.
