On a helpful note...

Da1, make sure your steam internet settings are at >10M, this was an issue I had for a while, game would randomly lag in full servers or intense firefights. Its steam capping your bandwidth usage, which is retarded, but just the way it works...

Also, older games don't like SLi and Crossfire, so if you are running dual cards (idk if you are) go into your driver and disable it for hl2.

Hope this helps!
