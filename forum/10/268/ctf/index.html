No need to download a mod...although there is a CTF mod, many have made CTF maps for vanilla HL2DM. The CAL CTF division is a HL2DM division and will use such maps.

Just look for maps that have been made with capture the flag type objectives. The easiest way is to sort your server list by map name and jump into a map that starts with a prefix of ctf, or hang out on servers run CTF oriented clans such as HAX, Nookie etc.
