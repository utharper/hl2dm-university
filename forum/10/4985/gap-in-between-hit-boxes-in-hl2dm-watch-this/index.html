The shots fired are unlikely to come before or after anything. The game calculates bullets as trace lines which all will take place in the same frame. Back in HL1 there were demonstrations that bugs in the game would cause odd issues. They could take an AWP and fire it at a line of players and sometimes not all the player would get hit. Meaning somehow a single traceline had not calculated a hit on a player. It would not surprise me if these still exist, it would surprise me if anyone at valve went through extra work to write code for a shotgun that fired pellets at different speeds. Trace lines are instant they have no velocity or infinite velocity depending on how you want to think of it.
