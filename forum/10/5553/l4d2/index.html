I've put a good amount of hours into L4D2 since I got it on sale for eight bucks. I bought L4D1 too but never really played it.
The weekly mutations are fun. Does L4D1 even have those?
Most of the fun VS matches happen on No Mercy, Dark Carnival, The Parish, and occasionally Dead Center. All the other maps are either too hard or too easy.
All of the campaigns are fun to play on expert if you have a good group. They're certainly challenging enough and leave you feeling accomplished when you beat their finales. Not because the characters made it and the (non-existent) plot can move forward, but because you used teamwork to accomplish your goal.
Playing single player is useless and boring because the AI is terrible.
Melee is integrated just fine, it's not OP or UP.
The new infected are really fun to use IF your team knows how to work together.
Seriously who the fuck pays that much attention to the characters? It's not an RPG here, just shoot the zombies...
