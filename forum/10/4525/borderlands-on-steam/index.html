Adjust FOV

Bindings=(Name="F11",Command="FOV 110",Control=False,Shift=False,Alt=False)

to turn voice chat off (it's voice activated by default)
open willowengine.ini in the same directory

find bHasVoiceEnabled=true

Problem hosting games (some people needed to do this, others, not at all.. weird.)

like many other online multiplayer games, requires that you forward a few ports if you plan on hosting a game. This basically means that if you want people to join your game you need to open up a path in your internet connection for them to get there, otherwise you'll be left to loot all by your lonesome.

But don't worry! This is a pretty easy thing to do!

First, determine whether or not these ports are already open
There are some cases where port forwarding is unnecessary, such as when you connect directly to a single port cable or DSL modem.

To test for this just click each of the following links, it will tell you if the port is open or not.

7777
28900
27900
28910


If the ports were closed, we need to determine what kind of router you're using
You'll need to find the model name of the router or router/modem combo you're using. This shouldn't be too hard. Usually, the internet comes in through your modem (box connecting to the wall either through phone line or cable line) and is then passed to a router which sorts and distributes your internet connection to the computers in your house. Go find the latter, turn it over, and look for a brand name and some kind of model number (Ex.: Linksys wrt54g, D-link DI-804). Once you have that go to this link and locate your model:

http://portforward.com/

Follow the instructions provided. The ports you need to open are:

7777 (TCP/UDP)
28900 (TCP)
27900 (UDP)
28910 (TCP)

Once you're done click each port to test if it's really forwarded. Bear in mind that sometimes it takes a minute for the router settings to apply.

Some people tested some weapons over and over and found out why oranges sometime appear to be shittier than greens/blue/purple...

A weapon can have up to 5 stats, the game as it is now, only shows 4 lines, and those 4 are sometime used to the weapon description.

Example of what the stats of a weapon should be:

Annihilate 'em bandits
10% ignite
-20% recoil
40% crit damage
20%faster reload
Regenerate ammo

And what it is ingame now:

Annihilate 'em bandits
10% ignite
-20% recoil
40% crit damage

Amo regen and reload speed are two really good mod, yet you dont even know they are there.

to fix it, make the ingame font smaller (they really needed to be smaller anyway)



Go here and open it with notepad and change the first line after general globals blah blah blah to look like below

[General.Globals GlobalsDefinition]
AttributePresentationTranslation="<font size="10">$NUMBER$ $CONSTRAINT$ $DESCRIPTION$</font>"
