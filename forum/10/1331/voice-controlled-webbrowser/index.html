What You Need:

Opera Web Browser: You can get it here http://www.opera.com/
A Microphone

Steps

1: Click the voice tab and install it
2: Then click on Tools then Preferences and then click the Advance Tab. You can also change what button to press for voice activation.
3: Then click the edit button and then application. There will be a large list click New
4: Then type in Overclock.net or any other name you wish to call it.
5: Then double click next to it in the actions column and type/copy this * Go to page, http://www.overclock.net * (without the * )
6: Click ok and you're ready to go.

Recent Opera, well for me anyway got rid of the button to download the voice software so...to get this go to tools > preferences > Advanced > Voice and check the box that says enable voice activiation and youll be able to download the software.

The joy. May you feel it.
