Back in the early days of HL2DM, well before even Cynosure was around, I discovered my love for electronic music. In an effort to express this (and impose my preferences upon the world), I put together a dense mix of some of my favorite tracks of win, lovingly dubbing it (of course) the win_mix. As a result I made somewhat of a name for myself in my little gaming circle as an "enjoyer" of obscure, under-appreciated electronica, a perception I have sought to perpetuate through the win_mix's two sequels.

It only follows, then, that I would bring myself to create a fourth, which, in keeping up with tradition, I introduce to the old HL2DMU stomping grounds. Now, it's been quite a while since my HL2DM heyday, and I doubt any of you remember me, but don't let that stop you from grabbing the 123 MB .rar file filled to the brim with excellent music you never even knew you liked.

And with that, my work is done (for now). Enjoy!

-tFlow
