basically It's pretty much like COD but here...
Player levels unlocks perks
Blood Diamond (money) is to buy...

Weapons
Customize, Repair, or get brand new weapons

Buffs
small, medium, large XP boost for a given time.
smal, medium, large blood diamond boost for a given time.

Appearance
Silly googles, biker boots, clown facepaint, gasmask, cigar.... anything to look like you want to look.


Gameplay is pretty much like the COD series, mixed down with a lil CSS.

Deathmatch
Team Deathmatch
Territorial
small maps are part of a bigger map, these small maps you play matches on has routes and paths in them, depending on who wins the map and HOW? (place the bomb = unlock a path next round in your favor, or kill the other team for blood diamonds)

Ultimately the winning team CAN be based on frags but mostly relys on who's in control of the bigger map.

Not my cup of tea (i dont play FPS that has ironsight and spray control etc...) but if they play their cards right, it could beat combat arms.
