custom made laptops are always best when it comes to getting performance out of them like alienware or dell. Playing HL2DM is possible if the specs right and that u are willing to reduce the graphics quality. If u intend using hl2dm while traveling u might need to carry the adapter because it will eat thro the battery charge with in a hour or more. Usually when it comes to laptops u want to get the best spec u can because as said u can't really upgrade them so if u intend to not have buy a new one within a year or two then spec high. But i doubt u will need to if u intend to just use programs like hammer. But if u are like me that uses photoshop and other resource hungry programs 2gb of ram 2ghz duel core should be enuff plus 256mb graphics card that i would say should be the minimum u should get since that what i got 2 years ago for my dell so u should aim higher for today's prices.

also get a nice screen that is fair good size anything between 15-17 is good size. also my dell came with a option to get a longer life battery charge if u get that option get it. BTW i paid £1100 for mine for today i might get it for under £800 so really it should not be to hard to get a decent laptop for little.
