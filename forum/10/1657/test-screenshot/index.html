Resizing photos is very easy, if you have any photo-editing programs then all you've got to do is change it's Width and Height, the easiest way (Since you're uploading it online anyways) would be to go to http://www.imageshack.us - Upload your image, and click on the "Resize Image" button, 640x480 is fine.

An easier method of actually posting an image would be to grab it's URL (Right click the image, click Properties, then copy the URL.jpg), then paste it into your post, and put [img]URL[/img] tags around it.

You don't need to go through the trouble of attaching an image to DM-U.
