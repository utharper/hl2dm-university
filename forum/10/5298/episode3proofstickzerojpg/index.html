Going a few back, let me remind you Valve announced soon after the release of HL2 that it will no longer release full games in the series but focus on episodic releases, to quote them "so we can ship out new content in shorter time periods. instead of waiting 6-7 years for a game, you would wait 1-2 years for an episode". more or less these were the words.. .
Two things we know for facts with Valve: 1. they need to make economically driven decisions - so if something accumulates enough hype to becoming a hit then it's a good prospect. and 2. they do what gives them pleasure - this is what sets them apart from other developers.

BTW, i cannot honestly believe Valve "forgot" those EP3 entities in the AS SDK "by accident" and is now going "oh shit, now they'll be on to us". if you know anything about Valve, this is how they've been playing since forever. they love dropping you leads and hints everywhere.
