ShootMania Storm mouse sensitivity

Viperbird

2012-12-05 09:06:06

This is how to convert your HL2:DM/CS:S in-game mouse sensitivity to ShootMania Storm, in order to get the same "feel."

(Note: This is based off of my personal mouse movement in relation to the cross hair across the screen. I used the same distance on my mouse multiple times until I got both games to line up to be approximately the same. This is NOT 100% accurate, but its close.)

This doesn't factor in your mouse DPI/CPI, this is strictly in game sensitivity.

Multiply your HL2:DM Sensitivity by 0.153 to get the sensitivity for ShootMania Storm.

For example, my HL2:DM sensitivity is 0.4375, so using the above, my my sensitivity for ShootMania comes out to approx. 0.067

Tweak this a little to your liking, but this is a rough guide to get you started.

If you use acceleration then you are on your own.
