The most successful pub server in Europe, (Now I'm talking about successful in the way that players of higher lvl was playing on it all the time) was the Ambush server. This is why i think it was successful:

1. It was never locked.
2. It had never changed IP.
3. It had only a few maps, all well known by the community of a higher lvl.

Points one and two are kinda self explained. Point three in my opinion are a very important one, you knopw what to expect form that one server. You know you will never have to download or learn a new map. That everyone already knows the map will lead to better standard of the game. The rotation was strict Ld, bio, Lv. Kept like that. Most players found the standard of play so nice that even if you didn't love the map you stayed. There was a fight on the server, what else can you request of a public?

It was also no rpg for some reason, on the Ambush server. In some way I think this could benefit the server. Me myself don't care what ever weapon the opponent uses but there are some that does. Without the RPG you got rid of all the whine about rpgwhoring. Whit out that whine you get a nicer environment. Another pro with this might have been that without the RPG, you'd find much less map domination. What I mean with this is for example, you play Lv. There's 10 people on the server. There's this guy controlling the map or parts of the map with a RPG. With 10 people on the server weapons tend to not be where you expect em to be, resources are not too accessible. The guy that controls the RPG or a map with it will rtack up spawnkills and easy kills on people without any kind of equipment.

Getting spawnraped and raped where you have no items by someone who controls a part of the map doesn't give a positive gaming experience to newer players or oldfarts.

Not that this is wrong, it's as much a part of the game as doing a awesome head shot or summeth like that. It might contribute, it might not to have No RPG. It depends on what you wanna achieve with your server.

And btw, no freaking plugins. Plugins suck balls. Use rcon, and only on trusted peeps. Keep this a pub, not a server that you use for own needs.
