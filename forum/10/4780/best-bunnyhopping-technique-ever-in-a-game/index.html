HL2DM bunny hopping is terrible what are you crack heads talking about?

As said Quake started the bunny hopping it was a bug left in for a while, then altered in Q3. Bunny hopping was probably never intended in any major game it was just an artifact inherited from the quake engine which was the source of just about every FPS game ever made. They coded the movement to solve problems with games not allowing players to move correctly and then when added to air accel and bugs in friction while jumping people figured out the loop hole or exploit we call bunny hopping. Most companies at some point attempted to patch it out of the game, since most companies had noobs who could not bunny hop coding for them their patch attemts were like government laws which only partially fixed the problem and often led to more complicated solutions which still left loop holes, another tactic was to just punish the hell out of people for jumping like later CS. With HL2DM you just crouch to get around it surely no idiot coder did that on purpose but they were to lazy to patch it out once people were used to it. The early versions of HLDM and QW, 1, 2 had very nice bunny hopping. After that things went strait down the crapper and Mods would try to bring it back, some succeded.
