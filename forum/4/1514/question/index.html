If you want rapid fire pistol you'll need to bind both mwheel up and down, like so:

Code: Select all

bind "MWHEELUP" "+attack"
bind "MWHEELDOWN" "+attack"
It is possible to make a bind that will switch to the pistol and attack_wheel (thats what we'll call it) with one key. then you can make another bind to switch back to your normal mwheel config MWHEELDOWN=JUMP
