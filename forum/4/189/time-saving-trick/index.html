No, the purpose is to reset the gravguns attack cooldown, which is 0.5 seconds for both primary and secondary fire. By swapping to another weapon and back to gravgun, the cooldown timer is reset and effectively bypassed, allowing for much faster grabbing / throwing / punting of items and pickups.
