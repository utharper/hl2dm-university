2007-10-27 04:48:28

I asked neolinkster how to do the black outline, it's *really* incredibly easy:


Basically, in the clientscheme.res there's this section in it:


Crosshairs
{
"1"
{
"name" "HalfLife2"
"tall" "40"
"weight" "0"
"antialias" "0"
"additive" "0"
"custom" "1"
"yres" "1 10000"
"outline" "1"
}

Add the "outline" "1" value and it'll automatically outline your crosshair in black. Really cool.

I ctrl+F for halflife2 in the clientscheme and it gets me to that section in 3 tries (in case you can't find it)
