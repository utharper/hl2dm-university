Skaruts wrote:
Just what I needed to outline my white and not-so-good-for-bright-areas big dot crosshair.

.
In your Clientscheme.res file, find (under FONTS) crosshairs, and add
"outline" "1"
on the line after
"yres" "1 10000"

so it looks like

Code: Select all

Crosshairs
		{
			"1"
			{
				"name"		"HalfLife2"
				"tall"		"40"
				"weight"	"0"
				"antialias" "0"
				"additive"	"0"
				"custom"	"1"
				"yres"		"1 10000"
				"outline" "1"
			}
		}
Voila! black outline
