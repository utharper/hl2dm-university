CellarDweller

2008-12-05 17:24:15

killer, maybe make a demo on caverns... most folks here will have that map.

although i do practice strafe jumping and bhopping on the various jump maps... i like practicing more on the actual maps i'll be playing. helps to familiarize me with the maps overall.

i used to use lockdown exclusively. but caverns has some really good strafe jumps to practice. the easier strafe jumps to begin with are from the bridge to top rpg to the ledge to control... making a circuit you can practice over and over. also ramp to redroom, back and forth... makes you contend with a railing in mid jump. bathroom to front mag, back and forth.

the tougher strafe jumps on caverns are from front mag to top of supercharger over to balcony and back to top of supercharger. and also the jump from the bridge between front mag and crossbow room to the ledge over back shotty/mag. that one requires air control to bend around a corner. fun stuff.

there's also plenty of space to practice bhop timing as you move around the map.

my strafe jump has greatly improved practicing on caverns. my bhop still needs a LOT of work... i struggle with the timing. i have found it very useful to have a mentor on the server with you... someone who can spectate and give pointers.
