Gravjump: the folowing props are excellent to gravjump; Big crates (double crates), Small white pipes heater,toilet,sink. in order to get the maximum height stand on a edge of the prop you are standing on and look directly down on it, then primary fire yourself up the air, dont jump or you might totally miss or even damage/kill yourself. This is a good method for quick escapes and reach unreachable spots on a map.

Orb Jump: mostly used in dm_overwatch and seen in some vid by using a combination of 2 green cabinets, heres a better alternative,
1) go to xbow building
2) shoot/gravgun/get angry/yell at the HOTEL sign until it falls down
3) take that sign and bring it in the area you want to go very high.
4)place it on the sidewalk in order to half half of it over the street and the other half placed flat on the sidewalk.
5)Stand on the sign where its on the sidewalk
6) fire and orb and get it in your gravgun (non gravgunned orb will fail to make you jump high or might even break the prop, gg hotel sign)
7)shoot that orb on the part of the sign that is over the street.
