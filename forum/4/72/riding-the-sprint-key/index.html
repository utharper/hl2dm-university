Isolate your strafe jump. Practise the jump and aim to use two bars of aux for each one. After you execute the jump, just hold down duck and land ducked, no other keys. Don't worry about overbouncing, just land ducked and as soon as you have recovered your two bars, repeat.

Drop two...gain two...rinse repeat.

Once you can do it fairly consistently add in the overbounce. You bounce until fully recovered, then blend in another jump where a bounce would go.
