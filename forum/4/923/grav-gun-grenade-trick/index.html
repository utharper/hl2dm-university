Three nades in 2 seconds is a bit unrealistic, but you can them up pretty fast depending on how long you need to hold them. You can do it faster manually than any script, so no, no scripts.

Check out the video tutorial in our training vids section. Really it is just practice and perfect timing. I've had people who have accused me of scripting, but really they just didn't realize how easy it is after some time doing it.

A good way to practice is to set nade as default weapon, and then fire off two every time you die. Once you get advanced I recommend grav gun as default thereafter. If you need any help with default weapon, let me know.

It's a lot of practice and timing. Most people have their nades close to their grav guns with "binds", keys to switch fast. Mine for example are G and T, g for grav gun.
