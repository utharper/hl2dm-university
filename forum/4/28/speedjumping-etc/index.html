Try just sprinting on the ground and curving your mouse until you reach a high vel like 430ish and then combine that with just jumping

Do it over and over are you have the basic strafe jumping motion, which then you can keep on practicing until you master it and then you could combine that with more advanced techniques.

Another thing you can do to understand the strafing part better is to create a server on overwatch and set the grav to 0(sv_gravity 0). Then jump and wait till you hit the skybox. Then try to gain as much speed as you can by strafing around (Hold foreward and a strafe key while turning your mouse the same direction).Just try not to crash into the invisible walls on the sides of the map.
