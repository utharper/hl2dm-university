k well people been asking since i made a couple of vids how i jump so high with the objects.

Ready?

------------------------------
The only props you can fully trust at 99% of launching you in the air are:

+The square shaped grey heater

+The thin white heater pipes

+Sinks (lockdown bathroom for example)

+Toilets
-------------------------

Beside the above any other props tend to launch you only an inch in the air or you will might get hurt.

The more polys the prop has, the better it is, that's why cabinets or crates rarely works.

Toilets and sink will most likely throw you very high but you dont have much control on where you want to go since those props are pretty small and you can't run over them.

Toilets and Sinks are good at going straight up, not far.

They can be used, for example, to reach overwatch's RPG while gravjumping from the street.
*****
*****
Heaters:

Heaters rarely throw you damn high but they get the job done, sometime tho they can launch you as good as sinks or toilets.

Heaters are badass because
1) It's probably the best prop in the game considering it's
damage // velocity // weight ratio
2)Since you can run over them you can decide of an angle or at least a direction you want to take during your jump.
-------------------------------------------

Q: BUT CONFLICT HOW DO I JUMP WITH THEM ASDASDASDASD

A: In order to do that properly, one must stand on the prop and LOOK STRAIGHT DOWN and make sure you stand on the prop "edge"...the side of the prop.

Once you get that down try running over the props and look down at the right time to launch yourself.

Shouldn't take more than an half an hour to get the hang of it.

Several factors can affect grav jumping such as:

Floor in angle.
If you don't look TOTALLY down it might not work at all.
If you crouch on the prop.
Luck / BS


Hope that helped!
