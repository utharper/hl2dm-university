Nade leap.

provost

2007-03-20 17:49:20

With at least 100-15 armor, secondary fire a nade in front of you.
let the timer go down and when its about to blow up strafejump over it, you want it to explode behind you but not too far, not too close too or you'll be taking too much damage.
With proper timing and practice you'll be able to do a fast, major forward leap.
(example:from green car spawn to white car spawn in dm_overwatch)

...leaving you with sumting like 40 hp so use it wisely still can come in handy.

maps where its good to use; Powerhouse(RPG LEAP) Avalon (fast travel, theres lot of hp around) Lostvillage:(see avalon)
