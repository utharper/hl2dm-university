provost

In most maps there are plenty of usefull ricochet to be found.

RICOCHETS: a ricochet is done by firing xbow at angle on a solid surface such as a concrete ceiling wall or floor (and much more but you get the point)

BOUNCE: Bounces, or Orb Ricochet works exactly like xbow ricochet's but with an orb instead.

Technique: every bounce/ricochet has a start point and a destination point.
note that orbs are tricky and its tough to go for a precision shot but they cover more space and are deadly while xbow ricochets require precision.

Now, what i'm suggesting here. whenever you find a ricochet that you think would be useful, no matter how popular how simple it is good as long as its not posted here, no matter how obvious/easy/hard this shot is.

Way to do it should look like this:

1)Hi this ricochet is done on dm_lostvillage and it works like this (short desc)

2)*pic of starting point* (your crosshair will be exactly where the person has to shoot and a short description on where to stand) to host your pictures i recomand photobucket.

3)*pic of destination point* Where will it land and in wich situation its good to use this ricochet..

i'll post some as soon as i get back home, have fun studying the maps you like the most be original and creative
