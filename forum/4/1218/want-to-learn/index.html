As walking target bhop is really important, but most important than bhop IMO would be strafe jummping ( the initial jump you do to bhop correctly ).
The faster you get with the initial jump, the better it is.
Apart from this, when gravity gun jumping/props jumping/whatever you wanna call it, try aiming DIRECTLY DOWN towards the object.
Some people say you need to try with edges rather than trying with the middle of the object...
There is a thread about his posted by .conflict some time ago with tips like this, try looking for it, cuz it had some relevant info.
