called: dm_bk_ExAnimalis_rc2

build for gravitys around 500 +/- 100 (500 is a good value for the jumps in this map)

and has....

16 spawnpoints
8 lifts / with lil differences in lift-speed n jumping possibilitys /
5 rotating platforms / 1 on upper center platform + 4 satellites /
4 speed-tubes / (sry..working but still lil buggy at the moment.. hope 2 fix them soon)
4 ballspawner / each with 1 ball and a 80 sec. respawn-time /
3 ladders /
2 teleports / both in steady random mode with restricted access by rotating door-rings (blocking them will hurt u) + some audio-visual teleport-effects
1 supercharger / supercharger is protected by a energy-shield (will hurt u on touch when active)

buttons:

button 1: / switching off/on the rotating platforms
button 2: / disable superchargers energy-shield for 6 secs (means a max of 100 suitpoints on 1 charge..tricky .)
button 3: / disables upper platforms floorshield for 15 secs
button 4: / switching off/on both plasma beam effect in maps center
button 5: / for switching off all 4 ballspawners
button 6: / for open the supply unit device , 20 sec delay till closing again


weapons:

stunstick
crowbar
slams
grenades
3X 357
3X x-bow
5X shotgun
5X ar2 (3X orbs)
lots of physics (BK+A custom phys)
and new .) 5X [BK] custom skinned explosive barrels
