hey Skaruts, I've researched about this a bit here and there.
there is no one-click-flawless converter. the operation is quite the pain in the ass but doable.
UnreaEd and Source work exactly the opposite; Source is made out of World geometry brushes (solid matter) in a void while UnrealEd is a solid matter you "carve" chunks off to make your level. not to mention entities, materials and models are altogether different.
The way to go about this is to find (and i'm still looking for) a professional Maya or any 3D editor that will take a UT level's geometry static mesh and convert it using the 3D editor. then, it can be safely converted to Source.
as i said, a pain, but doable. just needs a 3D guy who's willing and able.
