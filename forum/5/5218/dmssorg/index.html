Dm_SSorg (Shortened of SpaceShip.org)

This map was designed from an idea in my head about having a deathmatch map in a spaceship. A heap of inspiration came from the game: DukeNukem 3D where a level of the game involves you to be on an alien spaceship.
I made this for my own pleasure, however I still wanted people to enjoy it on the public servers (tournaments did not come to mind then).

The layout consists of a series of corridors connected to medium-sized rooms, splitted by two levels. This is a highly close combat map, therefore it would be fast gameplay (that is of course if you can find anyone).

The RPG is at the back of the spaceship in a 'garbage compartment' which acts as a trap to suck out the RPG'er into space. This is activated by either one of 2 buttons: one at the front of the spaceship shown near a live rotating camera or at the back, right in front of the doors.
