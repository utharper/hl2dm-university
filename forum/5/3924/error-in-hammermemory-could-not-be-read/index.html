The problem may becoming from one of the following :

Windows - anything modified or recently may have been in your windows explorer? is your Windows theme modified? any missing / problematic windows updates? can also be some application installed on your machine that can rearrange paths or file assignment. Are all window components including Net Framework 1, 3, or even Java properly installed on your machine?

or Steam - do you have Steam installed on D drive? or anything of Steam installed on a drive other than C:\ ?
Go here and run ALL THE TESTS in the Specific tests section. note that running these tests at different times might reveal a problem that emerges after a while of the machine working, etc.

corrupted or unreachable file - Hammer will be having problems with this.
if so, look at your Hammer options to make sure you got all your paths pointing to the right direction.

CPU - any weirdness in the PC's operation at all? slow rebooting processes? hanging or acting funny on Windows Explorer tasks? freezing on a big app launch while other apps are opened? taking a while to "recover" from playing a heavy game or even DM?
