2008-04-06 20:51:23

My first map for HL2DM (or the Source engine in general) was dm_resident. It remains the only HL2DM map I ever released to a "finished" state, most others are lying around as betas, some more evolved than others. A later HL2DM update caused an ugly bug involving disconnected rope-physics. Do those lamps in powerhouse still lie on the ground? So little love for HL2DM from Valve's side.

The next big release was the overgrown dm_3006 which I consider to be more an experiment in theme-choice and HDR-lighting than a layout-driven map. Also I had to learn about an ugly func_tracktrain bug the hard way. The VMF for this was lost in a crash, also, so I doubt there'll ever be a final release.

Currently I'm finishing up dm_balcony, probably my most all-round working map so far. I tried doing something smaller or at least more medium-sized with this and get good connectivity.
