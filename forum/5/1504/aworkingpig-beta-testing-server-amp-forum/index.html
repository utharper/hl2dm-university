hi all, I would like to promote
Testing SERVER & FORUM



Background:
This server has been running for almost a year and was set up solely to test beta maps.
There is NOW a corresponding forum available for people to post submissions and to receive feedback on the maps.
The majority of the maps on there are still mine but we have had submissions from some talented and not so talented mappers, so long at the map is playable you are welcome to some server time. Although it only needs to be playable to be added to the rotation, i can still add it to the maplist for testing.

Opportunity
The server is yours, if you are a new mapper looking for somewhere to host/playtest your map
or an old hand wanting an alternative location and feedback opportunities
I think we all know players are the ones that make maps good.
Its easy to use to, simply sign up HERE and make a post in the submission section.

The Rules:
  • 1. You must start a feedback post in the forum with pictures.
    2. There is a No Leak Policy - ( I will help out here if I can)
    3. There must be a minimum of 8 info_player_deathmatch's
Testing:
Submitted maps are placed on daily rotation.
We also have a steam community group and schedule events for the 1000+
strong members where new beta's are given sole use of the server.

Hopes
I am hoping to build a small community of mappers and players that will help inform each other about how good maps can and should be made and also attract and encourage server admins to make use of the finalized maps.



Well, there you have it people. I hope to see some new faces on the forum helping us mapper to craft and form new maps and in the server, finding those bugs and adding those small tweaks that every map needs.

Pig Out !!!

Some Suggestions:


Player_clip
i would suggest you continue player_clip'ing the sticky areas, this doorway is an example


Skybox
The skybox texture is a bit bland, and its easily remedied HERE
is a list of different skybox textures, just add the skybox
name under map/map properties/skybox texture name.
Also look at env_sun, they can really bring a map to life.

If your feeling ambitious you could add a 3D SKYBOX

Charger!?
i'd consider adding a health charger here and a suit charger
opposite, just seems to fit imho. although the map is super
fast so they may not get used.


off grid!?
These brushes dont seem to line up, not a biggie espcially as
you cant see it during play but if you can help it i would



these brushes overlap, funnily enough it didnt show up
in game but if you can help it it makes for better
mapping if you map clean and precise esp when problems develop
and your hunting for stuff


Detailing:
This map will be great im sure, but i would like to see more detailing,
there are some nice textures hidden away in hammer and you can
be creative with how they are used.

There are better bordering textures out there mate.


I would add a border to this entrance here just for looks


This light seems a little bright and/or out of place,
maybe a light source would fix it. Although they may not be
needed in the upper bit of the map.


this area I guess could use some more detailing, a problem i
often find is that if i dont have a theme i find it hard to begin
detailing. maybe think about your maps location in the world...


angled brush
if you added a brush like below it may help ppl fly out from the lift below,
i found that it pushed me straight up, could be fun!!??



weapon layout
personally i would only have 2 of each weapon in the map, you can guide players a little this way
and give your map some balancing, although if you really wanted
ppl to be kitted out i would suggest using a player_equip
