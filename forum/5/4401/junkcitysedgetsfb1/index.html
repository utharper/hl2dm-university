Hi Guys

I'm just about to launch my version of junkcity on the world and thought I'd show it off a little in here

I'm going to launch this beta 1 build publically on Thursday evening approx 8PM and it should be running on two TSF servers IPs 78.46.183.83:27015 (dedicated junkycity mapcycle based in Germany) and 91.192.210.68:27016 (UK) until then I'll be having some private tests just to make sure theres no sillies

The map is based on pig popper's dev version of junkcity (http://www.junkcity.org) but Ive made several new paths to the top of the map's buildings, moved a few weapons (such as the RPG is hard to get now) and a few more on the rooftops, otherwise the geometry stays the same but vastly different looks.

The style of the map is based on EA's Mirrors Edge for its colour scheme and building looks. I hope you like it - enjoy the screenshots...
