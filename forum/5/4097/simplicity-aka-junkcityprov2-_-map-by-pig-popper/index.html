Main Changes: JunkCity.org


Movement-
I have added Jump pads in the place of the ladders, if you can control yuor movement fairly well you can reach all levels using them plus reach other areas too.
I also added the ladders, using lasers, as i noticed some less skilled players had problems using the pads.

Weapons-
Traditional weapon placement + extra shotty
Custom (floating) explosive barrels

Style-
I wanted to use the idea of an enclosed junkcity, so i created this in kinda of misshaped box, I made a feature of teh weapon placements, and used a large drop for most of them.
Lasers, sprites & metal stylee.

Extra's-
Super Charger - Due to the ease of movement I added a 200 Suit Charger ni place of the health/suit charger on the rpg building.
Extra ledge - added an extra ledge opposite the orb teleport, and loaded it with a shot gun and power-ups to help balance that dead area.
