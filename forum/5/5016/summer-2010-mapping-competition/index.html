CONTEST RULES

1. The Contest is open to all persons, regardless of country, race, gender, or other identifying factors. The administrators and judges of the contest are excluded from this contest and may not under any circumstances produce an entry to be judged.

2. Judges may not discuss, release, or preview any map entry with non-judges. Judges may not enter their own map or any map they helped create in any way.

3. The prize money is described in US Dollars. No warranties either express or implied are provided regarding the value in other currencies. International currency exchange rates that apply will be those determined on the day that prize money is distributed.

4. No warranties express or implied are provided regarding the amount of prize money available. Prize money will be distributed as follows: 50% of the prize money available will be given to the first place winner. 35% of the prize money available will be given to the second place winner. 15% of the prize money available will be given to the third place winner.

5. Any special mentions or runners-up will not receive prize money. They will, however, receive mention in the announcement of winners.

6. Decisions of the judging panel are final. No appeals may be made.

7. By claiming the prize money, the winner authorizes the use, without additional compensation, of his or her name in the announcement of winners.

8. In entering this contest, participants authorize the administrators and judges of the Contest to download, play, test, practice, and assess any map that participants provide. Participants authorize the administrators and judges of the Contest to discuss the maps and participants. Participants also authorize the distribution of a community map pack containing their maps.

9. By entering the Contest, participants state that they are able to legally receive payment via PayPal. Participants not able to receive payment via PayPal will be disqualified. In the case that a winner is not able to receive payment via PayPal, he or she will be disqualified and be replaced.

10. By entering the Contest, participants allege and impliedly state that they are the creators and owners of the intellectual property of the map they enter. Contestants found to have entered a map they did not create will be immediately disqualified.

11. Factors the Judges may Take into Consideration
A. The aesthetic beauty of the map
B. Suitability for Competitive Play
C. Arrangement and Number of Spawn Points
D. Arrangement and Number of Weapons
E. Suitability for Ordinary (Pub) Play
F. Lighting (must be LDR, NOT HDR or fullbright)
G. Average Frame Rate (FPS)
H. Any other Factors the Judges determine to be of Importance

12. Factors the Judges may NOT Take into Consideration
A. The age, sex, ethnicity, or personal disposition of the participant
B. The file size of the map
C. The name of the map

13. This Contest is subject to all federal, state, and municipal laws.

14. This Contest may be cancelled at any time, for any reason, without notice.

15. Map Entry Rules
A. Only one entry per participant will be allowed.
B. The map submitted must not have been released previously in whole or in part – it must be a wholly new map.
C. Contestants may enter maps as the product of a group effort. The group must decide amongst itself how the prize
money, if any, will be distributed. Only one PayPal account will be sent the prize money – other group members will have to
sort out the details.
D. Entry into the Contest will be through Private Message on the Half-Life 2: Deathmatch University Website. The Private
Message must be sent to either MondaySunshine or Valiums
E. The map entered must be a standard deathmatch map. No Co-Op, CTF, or RP maps will be judged.

F. The Private Message Must contain the following:
1. The entering contestant’s name
2. The entering contestant’s preferred in-game name
3. A link to where the map may be downloaded (in .bsp)
4. A short statement about why the participant believes the map should win (250 words or less, please)

16. After the competition is ended, winning contestants will be asked to provide PayPal details. If a contestant fails to provide
PayPal details within two weeks, that contestant will be disqualified and another contestant will be awarded the prize money.

17. The Deadline for submission is July 4, 2010. No maps entered after this date will be considered.

18. Any participant who releases their map to any server (for purposes other than strictly private, only-you maptesting) prior to the announcement of winners will be disqualified.

JUDGES

The judges are holymoly, Valiums, Edge, Confused, Cellardwellar, Blur, and Chemicalburn.
