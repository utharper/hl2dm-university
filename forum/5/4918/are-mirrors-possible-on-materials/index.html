ok... so I concluded that mirrors can only be achieved in the floor in this engine version. They work wonderfully, but never in a vertical side of the brush... Gets weird artifacts if done verticaly.

In the orange box it's easily achievable with a func_reflective_glass and a reflective glass material (which uses water properties aswell). (with particular attention to not have water or portals in a visible leaf)

A camera isn't an effective way, because you'd have to make the camera move in the opososite direction of the player direction (and at an equivalent speed) so that it could resemble the angles of a mirrored image. I dunno how that is possible...
