Ok, I decompiled it to take a look at on how they were made and my suspicions were correct. The problem here is the trigger_push entities placements. Fortunatelly it seems it all works well in the big long air tunnel. This is good cuz that tunnel would be a bigger pain for abnoksius to revise.

The big diference between both is that the big one actually has push triggers all along every tunnel and direction, while the small tunnels only have 2 triggers, one at entrance and one at exit. If something fails in between them you're screwed (It happens sometimes that you hit the round wall and instead of being conducted upwards you just stop).

So this has nothing to do with server/client stuff. It's all in the map.

However, it'd be good to know if anyone has had any problems in the big long tunnel.
