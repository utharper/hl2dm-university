That's a nice looking map really. You need to detail it alot more though. I dont see a single stain on anything and some walls look a bit bare. Also, you need to align your textures that are on the rounded corners.If you dont know how to properly align a rounded wall texture, highlight the texture right next to the texture you want to align on the rounded corner, then alt + right click that texture and it will line right up to the texture you have highlighted. You will then highlight the texture you just aligned and then alt + right click the texture next to it and so on and soforth. There is nothing worse then nice rounded corners with terrible unaligned textures. Makes me cringe.
