heh. yeah..i really liked AIR's spray so i asked him to use the name and spray on the map.

i'll post the map's specifics later on when i get home but for now; . ..

Map makes use of teleporters, floor jumpads and wall jumpads (horizontal).
Teleporters allow clients, props, orbs and grav nades in.
Sounds are also heavily used here allowing for better orientation as for which area you're in or functionality used..Supercharger sound, RPG pickup sound and so forth.

RPG - in the center of the map there is a Mudafuga logo on a wall lit in green. damaging it (shoot, crowbar, gg etc.) will set it off. wait 5 seconds and the RPG door will raise. The system then locks for 30 seconds.

A lot of extra care and time went into proofing this maps from game bugs like teleport issues, player and weapon spawns and the list goes on and on.

Map is fully clipped (re haymaker's comment about the walls) and that as well as all other features didn't show any loopholes through over a week of playtesting.

Map is currently at Release Candidate 5 so any input or newfound bugs are more than appreciated.

Thanks Shoobie and thank you all who participated in making this one happen. Working on this map was a lot of fun in many ways.

As i said above - Map's Specifics to follow.

I will post a DL link only when the map has reached final stage and that is because i'm seeing so many of my maps in beta stage running on servers. It's sad but nothing i can do about it.
