Tha Why:
This is a remake of the fantastic iceworld map. I believe Originally created by Fantasy for CounterStrike 1.6.
A blast from the past future stylee.. The reason i did this was to address some issues i had with the current versions namely, fullbrightness of the hl2dm version, weapons scattered everywhere effecting g-gunning stuuf and crossbow shots, the rpg was a little too easy to reach too, and general the whole styling which is a tad bland

Tha Design:
I have used elements from a map I was making with TheMisty1 based on Total Rekall, although there is no real theme to this map, more of a futuristic style.

Tha Game play
A big game play changed i have made was adding 5 jet pushes at the weapon locations, This can aid you in getting to the upper level where smg nades, ar2 orbs and the explosive barrels (also the floating suit charger) It also makes it a more dynamic map, you can really zip about if you catch the jet right..
I have also added 2 teleports at the magnum locations, jump in the tube and you get teleported from the upper level (pic 6)

Tha Weapons:
2x Magnum - 2x xbow - 2x shotty - 2x ar2
2 xSmg nade - 2x Orbs - 4x Explosive barrles

Tha Health:
no pick ups but there are 4 health chargers where there was once health packs, the give 10hp per second.
