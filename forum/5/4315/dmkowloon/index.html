I just had a little run around on your server and to get the cubemaps working properly you can do it all yourself. If you don't want to use the dm_kowloon.bsp I uploaded you can just copy paste it and rename it to dm_kowloon_r1.bsp and then build the cubemaps yourself.

That way everyone can play by just downloading that bsp from your server - and more importantly I won't have to upload another bsp to a bunch of sites

If you don't know how to create cubemaps you just open up a match with yourself via the "create server" option and then just type buildcubemaps into the console. The cubemap files will then be embedded into the bsp and you need just to restart the game for them to appear as planned.
