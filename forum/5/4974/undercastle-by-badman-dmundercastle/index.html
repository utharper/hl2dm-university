Interesting layout, not too repetitive so players don't get lost and I like the scatter of items and their unconventional placement, reminds me of Valar, who loves to hide items. I found the slope in the left of this screenie http://i42.tinypic.com/2s1kgoz.jpg bad for even a sprint jump, there's some other slopes that give the player a boost/lift up, but when on the run, that might slow me down. The supercharger is so easy to reach but a normal suit charger requires a jump hmm I'd rather see more stuff to throw around, like bricks, rocks, or a custom hatchet, or w/e medieval, than fight with orbs and smg nades.
I appreciate new ideas for getting the rpg, but yours is too similar to bio like you said, you even have an exploding barrel near it. And it doesn't quite feel like a castle, it's just a map with old rocky and some metal textures, maybe surround the map with castle walls? Or have castle traps like guillotines or closing gates to hurt the enemy behind me, or a trap door in the floor like in hanging places, or an iron maiden of some sort: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iron_maiden_%28torture%29. Maybe not functional, but those just being there creates an atmosphere, like in junkcity_begins or dm_wild_wild_west. Now it really depends on what you like and what you want with the map, but the layout could've just been done with all sorts of different textures. If you pick such a name and such textures, it's a pity not to take it a bit further, you know?
