I occasionally remake older maps and have been reprimanded (and justifiably so) for slapping our team logo on the walls. Problem is that I can't do anything about that now since the maps are already out there. It might be a good idea to have a map maker directory in the Uni where you can find the author behind any particular map. Naturally this has to be a central page, not forum posts.
  • EXAMPLE
    MAP: MAPNAME
    AUTHOR: MAPPER 1
    REMAKES: MAPPER 2, MAPPER 3, MAPPER 4 etc.

  • MAP: dm_magnum
    AUTHOR: gDawgg Moose
    REMAKES: [ELEA] Smacker Vino (dm_eleastyle_magnum, dm_wiremagnum)
