dm_TRACE | 100% complete.

Some of you have played this over the last few weeks, i have finally finished it now having added some sound and changed a few small details.

Another odd (movement) map, this is for the gliders amongst you. The straff keys are your friends, the doorways have pushes that pelt you up into the corners of this map, time it right and you can speed along the corridors grav gunning weapons as you go. Time it wrong and you end up plastered to the ceiling will someone snipes you form below.
The central corridor also pushes upwards and you can glide to the tower and grav gun the rpg if your quick.
All weapons are located on the upper sections, the map is scattered with orbs, smgs and magnums and a few shottys, xbows and grenades for good measure.
Health is given out at the base of central tower but you spawn fully loaded 100H + 100S.

This map is 24/7 for a little while, as ever play it here first
