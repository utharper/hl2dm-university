It's a shame the screenshots came out a bit darker than they should be, it is a bit of a dark map though as the theme is spooky and mysterious.

Also there are spinning powerups which will give you temporary advantages when you touch them, such as extra fast running speed and 100% heatlh boost.
The powerups will then respawn randomly at another location after a set ammount of seconds to ensure fairness and to prevent camping them.

Watch out for the wonkyroom, pressing Activate then going in here is sure to get you wonky! Be careful, this could spin you out!

Every weapon is included, with a puzzle to solve to get the orbs and rpg
As you play the map there is a lot of custom background noise and custom warped ambience effects and textures used all over.

I will keep you posted with news!
