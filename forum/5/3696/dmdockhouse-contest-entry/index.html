2009-04-15 05:53:17

You've got a good handle on hl2 mapwork. The execution is excellent, could be a chunk of SP hl2. GREAT detailing, little touches like the fishnet and the blocklight boat shadow really stand out. Add in the poor framerates though and I'd say you've got more work to do.

Big laggy water is usually a nono, but that's not the major cause of bad frames here. Going spec, the void outside the map is sky blue which I've seen on other badly performing maps. Dunno why that is. I checked with wireframe 3 and you have TONS of optimizing to do here, I'd say you could squeeze quite a bit more out of this. As it is you have no prop or item fades, no hinting or areaportaling. I can see everything from everywhere which is gonna be tough to fix given the layout. You might have to resort to occlusion in some spots as well.

I'm glad that this competition has attracted talented creators like yourself for this game, this rocks for the 'immersion' factor imo ( good sound work too btw ). Keep it up!
