This is dm_hope. A TDM domination map. Theme is c17, daytime.

Map is built out of a main street level area, a basement and two opposing courtyards, each on the other endmost of the map.
In TDM, each team spawns in its own courtyard and both aim to dominate the top floor in the main building right at the center of the map. Domination point has an RPG. That point is balanced by several high points, acting as snipes. All map’s areas are interconnected and movement was kept as fluent and as smooth as possible as optimizing the map allowed for.
The map plays in both TDM and DM.
I want to thank, AIR, Beef, Nino, MafYa DJ and ILM for their inputs but mostly to Impala whose suggestions greatly transformed and shaped where the map was going. So if you liked it – tell Impala. If you didn’t or have any input – tell me.

The map is in beta stage and therefore it would be best for server owners to watch this space for a final release.

Some good ol' explosive barrels. If anything, it might be too easy to get your hands on a good weapon for tdm, but I'm sure it's preferable if you want to run it in pure dm mode.

There is an obvious shortage of health packs, but maybe that's a good thing - at least close to the rpg it is.

ATM, I think it comes down to balancing out the two team's chances at getting to the various resources. The ar2 with the balls in the basement seems to be a key down there and one team has a great deal better chance of reaching it first. Not to mention that the other team will want to avoid going down that long passage to get there if there's a chance of balls coming flying. Have you balanced this out by making the opposing team's route to the rpg shorter - the ladder up one side of the rpg building I guess is a great advantage to get there.

So far, looking really good! Should be good with different number of players since it's so well connected, but still fairly sized.
