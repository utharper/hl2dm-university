So hi. I'm arena and this is my first post and first map for dm.


An overview of the map. as you can see the map is symmetrical, however weapon, prop, and spawn layout is not. All the textures were made by me except for the lava one from magma in SourceForts.



The middle of the map with the supercharger right in the middle. The block sizes are 32 64 and 128. you can jump from the small blocks to higher blocks. Some of the small blocks can be moved. Move all the small blocks into the middle lava pit for a surprise.


A view of one of the side hallways with the 357 visible. the little recess in the wall leads to a small area with some ammo (either shotgun or pistol) and a health and suit charger.


Just another shot with a movable block and a side lava pit. the slam is next to the shield chargers. Every weapon is available in this map.


A view from the middle there are hidden stashes of batteries and health hidden behind some blocks.


A shot of the middle lava pit.

So this map was originally made for SourceForts and was modified heavily to suit hl2dm more with a lot of help from thirst who plays a lot more dm then me and a few sf players. Hope you guys enjoy it. I left it as a beta since I thought you guys would have some good points of critique to help improve the map.
