This is the full release for dm_this_is_sparta now!


If played in a good dm game this map should be pure madness, hence the motivation for the name change. There will be plenty of moving targets with numerous jump pads, elevators, and teleports throughout. A large number of health kits, armor, and every weapon is represented except slams. An accessible RPG and supercharger are waiting in an underwater semi-timed section. This makes camping the top sides not recommended, although dominating with the RPG shoud be still proove challenging.

Changes since beta:
*Changed slope of inclines at end elevators - Orbs can be bounced through the shafts
*Added many physics objects
*Added RPG rounds
*Changed skybox - less distracting
*Added some decals


Changes since alpha:
*Improved lighting and visibility
*Performance Optimizations
*Placed player clip on combine glass so its not slippery
*Fixed crates and barrels not respawning
*Scale and sound adjustments

Only bug I am aware of is a shotgun pickup toward the center that cannot be moved with the g-gun.

Jelly Fox:
"The teleport for the RPG is too high, you should make it so you have to dive down to the bottom of the hole to get teleported. it would make it riskier to get too and wouldn't teleport you accidentally.

unclip the top parts either side of the two central lifts, would be cool to get up there...

could even get rid of the teleport and stick the rpg and charger up there"


I didn't want to allow players to get too far up (like to the parts where the inclines are, although I think you can if you prop jump). It would take the focus too far away from the central action and encourage camping. I also didn't want the rpg to be too hard to get at. If you do go for the rpg, and drain the charger on the way out, you can make it out without taking any drowning damage. But if you aren't perfect you are going to start taking damage. That also allows another player to simply teleport in and pistol whip you to death as you are drowning. Even though you just drained the charger and acquired an rpg. It' actually pretty dangerous to go for that and should provide some good pistol battles.
