This look's like a very nice map. I do however have a few suggestion's for you.

In screenshot 2: The ladder seems a bit short. I'm assuming your using the default 128 unit model, and it wouldn't reach that top shelf/prison bed w/e. Maybe make a ladder that fit's out of brushwork, or model one that fit's the height you need. The arguemnt could be made that it's lower half was broke over time ect. So it's not a big deal.

Screen 3: That lower area under the glass look's very nice. It gives the map a feeling there is more then meet's the eye. Maybe put some edging around this glass floor. (Caution strip, with concrete edge's ect)

Screen 4: Water sucks in MP map's IMHO. If you wanna leave it in just make sure you use a water_lod_control for ppl with low end system's.

Screen 5: The textures on the right side wall look stretched. Definately fix that. A small tip...when you texturing corner's, hold down your Alt key while applying the texture around the corner's.

The map looks great as is, just a few suggestion's
