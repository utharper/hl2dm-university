hello all.

after rigorous play testing of 1v1s and 2v2s with many many different players, I feel it is time for the obligatory thread on the U.

this is dm_ethic. it's not the prettiest map, that's for sure... but that's not the point. 99.5% of my focus was on gameplay. initially intended for just 1v1, i was pleased to fine that it also plays a fast and fun 2v2.

WARNING: THIS MAP IS NOT REALLY INTENDED FOR PUBBING WITH MORE THAN 6 PEOPLE THOUGH IT CAN KINDA WORK

the ramps you see in the image below are to be used kind of like a surf map... to stifle fall damage and increase the speed of gameplay. this map rewards players with good movement.



in the image below there is a jump pad, which allows players to jump up to the catwalks and collect the goodies.

the area with the canopy contains an rpg. the ramp in front can be utilized by strafing and jumping into it. it takes some practice to do consistently so beware
at first, many don't understand how to get the rpg. this is achieved by crouching in front of the box with skull and crossbones and gravity gunning underneath the gap.



there are many facets to the gameplay of this map that must be discovered on your own. i'll put up a link hosting the map soon.

valar offered to do final texturing for it, and swot offered some additional help, so this map is subject to (cosmetic) change.

hope you all enjoy.
