So this is my competition entry, dm_luno.

Info:
I started making it with no intention of entering it but as it progressed I thought what the hell and decided to submit it.
I guess it has the theme of some kind of warehouse/factory although I do intend to change the look of the map a little in the future.
I focussed on mainly making it suitable for 2v2 or 3v3 matches with gameplay being the highest priority however, I think 1v1's and 4v4's are probably playable here.
Valar has gave me some tips on making the map look better but I thought I would post this beta here now hopefully receiving some feedback in regards to the layout and weapon placement because I would rather sort that out before attempting to improve the map visually. Any suggestions for the map aesthetics are welcomed though.
Thanks to Shoobie and Pig Popper for help with various stuff!
