About:

The map has 8 hallways 4 on the lower level, and 4 on the upper level. On the lower level there are 2 stair ways to get to the 2nd level also 4 lifts and 4 ladders. The main area is the middle lobby this is a nice and open area. The weapon layout has been well thought about and the gameplay is fast. The lighting took along time to get just right.
