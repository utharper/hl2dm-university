There are a number of ways to deal with this. Classically in most games people disable the teleport then push the player out of the way then enable it again so the next player can go through. It just takes a little extra work for mappers. Rotating exits is another option that is popular. This is another reason why updating maps is a good strategy I get chastised for often. Get updates which correct the problems with teleports and other issues out so a map can become better then it was before no mapper no matter how good makes a perfect map the first time improving it through many iterations makes it the best it can be for everyone from pubs to league play.
