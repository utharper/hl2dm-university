I do not know the answer but I can give you some advice. You will probbaly lose some information but look through autocads save as formats and see if anything exist in there that is more universal such as eps format. Also look at 3dsm and see what formats it can open. If you are lucky one will hit the spot. If that does not work try searching the internet for any relevant conversion. Autocad to maya, xsi or anything. Cause you may find a 3 step process which gets you what you need. In mapping we usually can switch to the map format to goto a different engine but most of the time you lose information so be prepared to rebuild parts of the model.
