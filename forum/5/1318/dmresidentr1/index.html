Hello ! =D

With Cartel, we started to take dm_resident as homemap, we found this great !
We are in div. 2 on CU and we wanted to take this as homemap instead of dm_lostvillage.

Unfortunately, CU admins didn't let us having dm_resident so the afternoon they said "No !!", I started Hammer (that I didn't started for ages) and started to edit dm_resident. I made lots of versions (nearly 15) always asking to my team mates what to do anymore while testing new version. After lots of version, I said "Yes ! This is our new homemap !" so I posted it on CU forum but I forgot a quite big leak that eGz' Warez fixed. Now the final version is here, DM RESIDENT R1.

Now, after three months and half playing it. I bring my mod_map to you ! =D

What's news since the original dm_resident?

-Useless physics removed
-A secret room
-New weapon placement
-New health placement
-New respawns and 2 respawns deleted
-Some new ledges
-Ledge above de magnum room deleted
-Door and windows are bigger
-Chimney is bigger, you can now jump into this one and make some new tactics
-New camping point for new tactics
-More powerful light 'cause of a light problem in the d or e version (Fixed to realistic light)
-Game flow is now good and you can jump through the map fastly
-Nodraw texture to have a better file size (original : 4069Ko; pro with out nodraw : 5500Ko; final version : 4349Ko) and better FPS
-Etc..

This map is more competive and you can make more and more tactics on.
